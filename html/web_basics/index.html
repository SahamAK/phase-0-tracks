Why we need a dog?

Dogs are Humans' best friends. My children have been asking to get a dog for a while now. But their dad does not want to have a dog because:

  • he is allergic to fur.
  • a dog bit Him when he was a kid
  • we have carpet on the floor

He also have the following concerns if we get a dog:

  1. how long does it take to train a dog?
  2. who will walk the dog?
  3. how often do we need to walk the dog?
  4. who will walk the dog when it is freezing outside?
  5. do you think you will not argue who's turn it is to walk the dog?
  6. etc.
